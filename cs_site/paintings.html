Everything is a painting

Why? Because what is at the core of the average human experience? Our sense of sight and sound. We think of perception, we automatically think of seeing. In fact, it's so closely tied to experience we don't think twice about it. We put our money on beauty of people and objects and travel great lengths to see sights, we put alot of our trust into food that looks good, clothes that looks good, faces that seem trustworthy, and study the art of designing to balance function and beauty. For a moment posit that music is a medium of painting on the auditory wavelength. Tactile feeling is painting on material level etc. Repeat this disposition for everything you may come across. Merely an appearance of experience. What are you left with?
